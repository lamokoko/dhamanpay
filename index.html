FAQ

Questions users ask
before using DhamanPay.

Is my money safe?

Your payment is locked in escrow and only released after delivery is verified.

Do I need to trust the seller?

No. DhamanPay replaces blind trust with a controlled payment and verification system.

What if the item is wrong?

The customer can open a dispute and provide details such as serial number or video proof.

Who decides in a dispute?

The admin checks the delivery proof and dispute evidence, then releases or refunds.

When is money released?

Only after the delivery proof is submitted and the order is cleared for settlement.

Who uses DhamanPay?

Customers, merchants, couriers, and admins each use their own role-based dashboard.
